Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about InGenium and our services.

General Questions

What is InGenium?

InGenium is a software platform designed to help young entrepreneurs launch and grow their businesses by offering idea development support, business management tools, and financial guidance.

How can InGenium help my business?

We provide tools and resources for financial aid assistance, personal branding, content creation, and business management to help your venture succeed.

Who is InGenium designed for?

InGenium is designed for young entrepreneurs and small business owners who are looking to launch or grow their businesses with professional tools and guidance.

Account and Pricing

What are the pricing plans?

We offer three pricing plans: Basic ($9.99/month), Premiere ($19.99/month), and Business ($49.99/month), each with increasing levels of service and support.

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time through your account settings.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for all new users to test our platform and see which features best suit their needs.

Services

What kind of financial aid assistance do you provide?

Depending on your plan, we help identify and apply for grants, loans, and other funding opportunities at the local, state, or national level.

How does the AI content creation work?

Our AI tools help generate various types of content for your business, from social media posts to marketing copy, based on your brand voice and business goals.

What business management tools are included?

Our platform includes tools for task management, scheduling, financial tracking, and other operational needs to help you run your business efficiently.
